Red Skirt

Skirt made from a pattern. Waistband with front pleats and back darts. 3” kick flap. Edge stitched pockets.



Blue Skirt

Altered an existing pair of shorts into a skirt. Previous shorts were size 14. Took in sides to adjust the size. Sewed up the front and back to eliminate leg holes. Re-hemmed.



Orange Skirt

Designed and constructed without a pattern. Elastic 2” waistband. Layer of lining and two layers of chiffon for rich burnt orange color. All layers gathered and serged onto waistband.



Velvet Dress

Altered from existing dress. Re-sewed sides, sleeve width, and arm scythe to fit two sizes smaller.



Tailored Coat

Coat design based on an altered coat pattern. Altered the pattern to eliminate the fitted waist. Tailored the pattern to fit my body. Fully lined and underlined. Notched collar. Inseam pockets.



Coral and White Swimsuit

Designed and constructed without a pattern. Dropped leg line for vintage look. Sewn in bust pads.



Gray Swimsuit

Swimsuit made from basic swimsuit pattern. Dropped leg line for modern look. Sewn in bust pads.
